ScribGen: Generating Scribble Art Through Metaheuristics

Computer Graphics
Scribble Art
Metaheuristics


While many methods bridge sketches and images, few turn images into expressive scribbles. Our work explores this untapped potential using metaheuristics instead of traditional learning-based approaches. By analyzing various algorithms, we show they strike a unique balance between creativity and efficiency.
