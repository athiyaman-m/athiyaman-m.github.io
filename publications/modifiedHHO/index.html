Modified Harris Hawk Optimization Algorithm for Multi-level Image Thresholding

Meta-Herustics
Evolutionary Algorithms
Harris Hawk Optimization
Multilevel Image Thresholding


The article introduces a Modified Harris Hawks Optimization (HHO) Algorithm designed to enhance thresholding in grayscale images. Inspired by the hunting strategies of Harris Hawks, the original HHO algorithm has proven effective in solving diverse optimization problems. Building on this foundation, the proposed modifications aim to improve its adaptability and overall performance.
